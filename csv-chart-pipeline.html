3 Spot Outliers

Pipe to grep and sort to find the top spenders — classic Unix power, zero learning curve.

$ csv-stats sales.csv --json | python3 -c "
import json, sys
data = json.load(sys.stdin)
# Show columns with highest variance
for c in sorted(data['columns'], key=lambda c: c.get('std', 0), reverse=True)[:3]:
    print(f'{c[\"name\"]:20s} σ={c[\"std\"]:8.2f}  mean={c[\"mean\"]:8.2f}')
"

revenue              σ=   118.40  mean=   153.80
price                σ=    23.80  mean=    47.50
quantity             σ=     1.90  mean=     3.20

Reveal fields with the widest spread — your highest-variance columns are where the interesting patterns hide.
